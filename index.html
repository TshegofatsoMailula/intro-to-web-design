Welcome to Dinkwe Upholstery

Dinkwe Upholstery is a proudly South African business that specialises in manufacturing, repairing, and restoring high-quality furniture. Our experienced team has years of expertise in transforming old, worn-out furniture into beautiful and comfortable pieces that enhance homes, offices, restaurants, hotels, and other commercial spaces. We believe that quality workmanship and excellent customer service are the foundation of our success. Every project is completed using premium materials, modern upholstery techniques, and careful attention to detail. Whether a customer needs a sofa repaired, dining chairs recovered, or a custom-designed furniture set, we strive to exceed expectations by delivering products that are both durable and stylish. Our commitment to quality has helped us build long-lasting relationships with customers who continue to trust us for all their upholstery needs.

At Dinkwe Upholstery, we offer a wide range of products and services designed to meet different customer preferences and budgets. These include upholstered sofas, lounge suites, dining chairs, office furniture, headboards, ottomans, and custom furniture designs. Our skilled craftsmen work with genuine leather, premium fabrics, foam, and durable wooden frames to ensure every finished product provides long-lasting comfort and value. We also provide repair and restoration services that allow customers to preserve furniture with sentimental value while giving it a fresh, modern appearance. By combining traditional craftsmanship with modern design trends, we produce furniture that is both attractive and functional. Customer satisfaction remains our highest priority, and we carefully consult each client before beginning any project to ensure the final product meets their expectations.

Dinkwe Upholstery continues to invest in quality materials, professional staff, and innovative techniques to remain one of the trusted upholstery businesses in the community. We believe that every customer deserves furniture that reflects their personal style while providing comfort for many years. Our friendly team is always available to assist customers with product selection, fabric choices, colour matching, and maintenance advice. We welcome both individual and commercial clients and pride ourselves on delivering reliable workmanship at competitive prices.
